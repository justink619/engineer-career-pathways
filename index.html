So what is the TechStream?

It’s a guide (not a checklist!) to inspire and drive development for our technical people. It is designed to support individuals and managers in development discussions, development planning aligned to the 70:20:10 model and is intended to provide a consistent understanding across the different engineering teams at ResMed.

Impact and Influence
Each level of the TechStream is marked not only by increased responsibility, but also by an increased impact within a domain.

What does this mean for me?

  • Individual: At this level, you are focused primarily on being a valuable contributor, gaining experience and learning how to be impactful within your team.

  • Project/team: At this level, you are a resource for your Team or your Project, either as a developing domain specialist for your team, or as a generalist/problem solver for them. You should be able to lead smaller efforts coordinating with other members of your team and drive them to completion and work through tough problems and solve them independently taking in feedback from your peers and focusing on the outcome.

  • Across projects/Department: At this level, you have an impact across Projects or Teams. You are the go-to person for a larger group as a domain expert or generalist. You will lead cross-team efforts involving more people and drive them to completion and/or you will take on large challenges, working with diverse stakeholders in multiple teams to solve a problem that affects your department.

  • GBU/Domain: At this level, your domain expertise spans your GBU. You focus on the technology development strategy for your domain or GBU. You lead GBU-wide initiatives for commercial and strategic opportunities for your domain. You will lead projects that span departments and be responsible for solving domain-wide problems.

  • Company: At this level, your domain expertise spans the organisation. You will represent your domain in company and industry forums. You will be a go-to person across the company to solve very complex problems. You lead company level initiatives that are beyond your role.

How does it work?

We’ve aligned each of our Engineering levels (Eng, Senior, Lead etc) with growing your impact and broadening your influence at ResMed. Our engineers can influence other individuals, a project, many projects or a department, across a business unit or across their domain in the organization, or our company.

The levels are a pathway and are cumulative. For example, a Principal Engineer shows the behaviours and capabilities of a Lead Engineer, Senior Engineer and Engineer.

Core Competencies and Technical Mastery

At ResMed we have 4 core competencies that describe how we should work to achieve our goals and what we should focus on as part of our development. Using these, we’ve captured descriptions of the behaviours, impact and influence to define what we expect of our engineers. Technical Mastery describes the qualifications, experience and technical capability of our engineers.

Why do we have it?

Talent is a key driver of ResMed’s success. As a technology company having a visible and defined approach to technical stream progression is central to our strategy of attracting, motivating, developing and empowering the best talent.

Our career pathways describes three streams for engineers at ResMed; Project, Managerial and Technical.

To support the development and recognition of our technical people we’ve refreshed our engineering career pathways for the technical stream (TechStream).

Senior Principal Engineer

Technical Mastery

You have a breadth and depth of knowledge across technology, engineering(domain specific), industry practices and the regulatory landscape in your field.

You are a company level SME and thought leader in your industry, contribute externally at Industry events and committees, publishes journals or company white papers.

You understand your technology area end to end.

You champion and role model a systematic and data-driven approach to problem solving and decision making.

Ongoing development

You educate the organisation and work to grow the level of the company in your domain.

You are able to clearly communicate and influence change across the global ResMed business and with industry bodies.

You may support and advise CEO OP’s level decision makers in technology strategies.

Collaboration

You promote constant focus of technology strategy and product development effort onto the areas of highest value to end users.

You see past your discipline based POV, collaborate across the broader business to achieve goals.

You are focused as much on department and companywide issues as your own.

Innovation

You liaise with key stakeholders to advise and communicate benefits gained through technological advances and innovation.

You strive for simplicity in solutions, processes and methodologies.

You actively pursue company level initiatives that are beyond your role.

You are actively engaged in industry wide initiatives, and partner with corporate, research or academic groups.

Drive for Results

You balance business acumen with your technical mastery to nurture a culture that aligns technologies and product development expertise with broader commercial goals and objectives.

You holds yourselves and others accountable for the technical decision making in their area.

You proactively take responsibility for working with teams and driving a solution when any challenges are seen.

Principal Engineer

Technical Mastery

You have demonstrated key technical leadership on multiple projects of significant technology or business impact.

You are recognised as a SME in your particular area, with followership. You are a go-to person for mentoring and called on for solving problems in your domain.

You consider the interactions between systems in solving engineering problems in connected product solutions.

Ongoing development

Your technical growth is self guided and changes in response to global business needs and technology.

Collaboration

You are able to communicate a technical vision to motivate and foster agreement on technical strategy within the team, the department and with critical stakeholders.

You use your deep customer insights to builds connections between end user experience, new innovations and technology developments and the organisations technology strategy.

You collaborate across your global business unit to deliver end-to-end product solutions

Innovation

You collaborate to architect the strategic technical vision for your area.

You may represent ResMed to industry bodies; influencing industry regulations, standards and best practices.

You leverage your broad technology delivery experience to drive business, safety and technical risk management for ResMed.

Drive for Results

You may lead initiatives to align dependent product developments for connected patient care solutions.

You are able to translate your technical expertise into strategic and commercial opportunities.

Lead Engineer

Technical Mastery

You may have a relevant postgraduate qualification.

You have a depth of knowledge in a ResMed (or relevant) product system, or a specific area of speciality (knowledge domain) which is strategically important for your business unit (GBU)

You have demonstrated key technical leadership on at least one project of significant technology or business impact.

You are respected as a subject matter expert, within your department, for your product system or knowledge domain.

Ideally you have experienced both success and failure in a variety of engineering projects.

You anticipate and take leadership to resolve complex and ambiguous engineering problems.

Ongoing development

You maintain superior technical skills and mentor the development of these skills in others.

Collaboration

You are able to communicate the business risk and impact of technical results , and influence to ensure appropriate action is taken.

You have a deep understanding of customer needs and understand the business context of work happening within your department.

You collaborate across projects and across your department to leverage knowledge and experience to deliver against business unit roadmaps.

Innovation

You are able to balance risk in innovation, design and technology to achieve optimal project and commercial results.

You lead adaptation of business process to align with industry standards and best practises.

Drive for Results

You are accountable for robust engineering outcomes within your department for your knowledge domain.

You lead technical strategy for future developments for your knowledge domain.

Senior Engineer

Technical Mastery

You have experience in the complete development life cycle, including at least one project of significant technology or business impact.

You have product/process development or relevant experience in a regulated environment.

You are capable of solving complex design and engineering problems autonomously.

Ongoing development

You mentor and build capability in others.

Collaboration

You are able to tailor communication of technical concepts at the appropriate level.

You make design decisions based on a strong understanding of customer needs.

You work collaboratively across teams and disciplines to achieve project objectives.

You are influential within your team, building followership.

Innovation

You lead innovation in your project team and challenge other teams members to adopt new processes, technologies, materials or achieve new functionality in the development of new products.

You identify needs for improvements in engineering/business processes and support implementation of process changes.

Drive for Results

You are responsible for the design and documenting of complex sub-systems/processes.

You are responsible for ensuring your sub-system/process meets the architecture and interface requirements of your system.

You provide technical leadership for your engineering discipline within your team.

Collaboration

Drive for Results
